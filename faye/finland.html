Kakslauttanen Arctic Resort

About

Kakslauttanen Arctic Resort is an extraordinary destination located 250 kilometers north of the Arctic Circle in Finnish Lapland. Since 1974, this family-run hotel has been offering year-round magical experiences in the pristine arctic wilderness. They are world-renowned for their iconic Glass Igloos and the magical Northern Lights, visible eight months a year.




How to get there

If you arrive by plane to Ivalo, the most convenient way to get to Kakslauttanen is to take our own airport transfer bus. Our driver will be there to greet you at the airport with a Kakslauttanen Arctic Resort sign and drive you directly to the resort. The trip will take approximately 30 minutes.
