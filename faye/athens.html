The Acropolis, Athens

About

Perched above present-day Athens, the Acropolis draws you up and in. Follow in the footsteps of ancients as you walk up the same steps that have been walked on since 438 BC - 2,500 years. Views out over the city are incredible as you walk between the meticulously restored ancient buildings. Near the end of the day, you'll want to linger and watch the sunset from the stairs near the entrance. This is a nightly ritual in Athens.The site is also impressive looking up at it from the city below. Spend an evening dining on a rooftop patio to soak in the view of the hilltop ruins lit up at night.

How to get there

The approximate time from the Athens International Airport to the Hotel is 1 hour. You will board the Metro from the Airport’s Station (blue line) and get off at Syntagma Station. At Syntagma Station you switch lines in the direction of Elliniko (red line) and get off at Station, “Syngrou-Fix” or “Acropolis”. To access the hotel on foot, take the metro to “Acropolis” station. Ascend Dionysiou Areopagitou pedestrian street and then turn left on Garibaldi street which leads to Mousson street. A closer station but more steep to walk is the Syngrou-Fix metro station. When you get off at this station, take the exit “Drakou Street” and walk up towards Filopappou Hill. You will find Mousson Street right in front of you. You then need to turn left and the hotel is located on your left hand side.
