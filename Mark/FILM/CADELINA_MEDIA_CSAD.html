The Power of Habit







"Existence is suffering" is the common translation of the First Noble Truth of Buddhism. As an avid reader of Zen Buddhism, I’ve often argued that this Noble Truth isn’t true at all, or that it is simply a precursor to the other Noble Truths which teach one how to alleviate suffering. But recently I’ve changed my stance.Lesson here: Would you sleep in a train station in order to achieve your dreams? If the answer is no, then you need to start changing your mindset to a “do whatever it takes” attitude.
