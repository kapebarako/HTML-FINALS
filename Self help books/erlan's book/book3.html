Atomic Habits

by James Clear


No matter your goals, Atomic Habits offers a proven framework for improving--every day. James Clear, one of the world's leading experts on habit formation, reveals practical strategies that will teach you exactly how to form good habits, break bad ones, and master the tiny behaviors that lead to remarkable results. The issue isn't with you if you're having trouble changing your habits. Your system is the issue. Bad patterns replicate themselves not because you don't want to change, but because you're using the wrong change mechanism.

Clear is known for distilling complicated concepts into straightforward habits that can be adapted to everyday life and work. He uses the most well-established theories from genetics, psychology, and neuroscience to construct an accessible guide to make good habits inevitable and poor habits impossible. Readers will learn a lot along the way. If you're a team trying to win a championship, a company looking to redefine an industry, or a person looking to stop smoking, lose weight, minimize stress, or accomplish some other goal, Atomic Habits can reshape the way you think about change and achievement, and give you the resources and techniques you need to turn your habits.
