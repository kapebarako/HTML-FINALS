the 7 habits of highly effective people

Review by Binasahan Erlan klaud G.

May 09, 2021

When Stephen Covey first released The Seven Habits of Highly Effective People, the book became an instant rage because people suddenly got up and took notice that their lives were headed off in the wrong direction; and more than that, they realized that there were so many simple things they could do in order to navigate their life correctly. This book was wonderful education for people, education in how to live life effectively and get closer to the ideal of being a ‘success’ in life.

There are two major issues here, and we're just talking about people who have already read the novel. The first issue is that most people are too lazy to put Stephen Covey's ideals into practice. The second issue is that many people have a limited understanding of Covey's values. There are people who have already been wowed by the novel. That's what we're aiming for. We're attempting to demonstrate how Covey's novel, or more accurately, his model, was a complete model in and of itself. There was nothing wrong with it. There should be no part of your life left unnoticed if you put it into action. The only requirement is that you comprehend these concepts and attempt to put them into practice throughout your life.
